Senior SAP ABAP Developer

Sunninghill, Sandton Full Time R123 - R456

Job description

As a Senior SAP ABAP Developer, you will be responsible for the design, development, and implementation of SAP ABAP solutions to meet the evolving needs of our business. You will collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to understand requirements, architect solutions, and deliver high-quality software that optimizes business processes and enhances overall efficiency.

Responsibility

  • Design, develop, and maintain SAP ABAP programs, reports, interfaces, conversions, enhancements, forms, and workflows (RICEFW) in alignment with business requirements and best practices.
  • Collaborate with business stakeholders to gather and analyze requirements, provide technical expertise, and propose innovative solutions.
  • Lead technical design sessions and provide guidance to junior developers on complex development tasks.
  • Perform code reviews, debugging, and troubleshooting to ensure the quality and performance of SAP ABAP applications.
  • Develop and execute unit tests and integration tests to validate the functionality and integrity of developed solutions.
  • Stay updated on the latest SAP technologies, trends, and best practices, and proactively identify opportunities for process improvements and optimization.
  • Provide technical support and guidance to end-users and resolve issues in a timely and efficient manner.
  • Participate in project planning, estimation, and risk management activities to ensure successful project delivery.

Qualifications

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field. Master's degree preferred.
  • Minimum of 7 years of experience as a SAP ABAP Developer, with proven expertise in designing and developing complex SAP ABAP solutions.
  • In-depth knowledge of SAP ABAP programming language, including ABAP Objects, ABAP Dictionary, ALV reporting, BAPIs, RFCs, and Web Services.
  • Experience with SAP development tools such as SAP NetWeaver, ABAP Development Tools (ADT), and SAP HANA Studio.
  • Strong understanding of SAP modules such as FI/CO, SD, MM, and HR, and their integration points.
  • Experience with SAP Fiori/UI5 development is a plus.
  • Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
  • Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced, dynamic environment and manage multiple priorities simultaneously.
  • Proven leadership skills with the ability to mentor and guide junior developers.
